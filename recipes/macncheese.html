Mac and Cheese

Make our wonderful homemade mac and cheese!

this is the best mac and cheese ever. I make it every Thanksgiving.

ingredients

  • noodles
  • cheese
  • milk
  • heavy cream
  • spices

Steps to cook

  1. boil noodles
  2. Mix wet ingredients
  3. add cheese sauce to noodles
  4. bake at 400 for 20 mins
